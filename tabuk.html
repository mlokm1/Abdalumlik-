Tabuk is one of the largest cities in northwestern Saudi Arabia, and it is located seven hundred kilometers north of Medina. Tabuk is an important agricultural region in the Kingdom. The city of Tabuk is characterized by its moderate climate in the summer, and therefore it is an important tourist destination where tourists enjoy beautiful beaches and dive to see the picturesque coral reefs. Tabuk is also distinguished by the presence of high-rise mountains that are covered with trees, making them stunning landscapes.
